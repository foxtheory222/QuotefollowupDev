# Quote Detail UX Review

The Quote Detail side panel should let staff act without losing their place in the queue.

Required sections:

- Header with quote/source document, customer placeholder, total value, and close button.
- Summary grid with status, assignment status, attempts, next follow-up, last followed up, TSR, CSSR, and branch.
- Prominent sticky note near the top.
- Quick actions for call, email, customer advised, next follow-up, roadblock, escalation, won, and lost.
- Action history timeline.
- Source quote and quote-line references.

Sticky note placement near the top is mandatory. It is the persistent work item memory and must not be confused with action history notes.
